Evaluation of AI-generated paleo-ecological reconstructions

Welcome! This page contains pollen data and visual reconstructions generated using AI. Please review the material below before proceeding with your assessment.

Research Context

This survey is part of a PhD research project aimed at exploring and better understanding the relationship between humans and non-humans, observing how it has evolved over time.

Methodology

The research framework integrates a predictive modelling phase to analyse ecological dynamics, which serves as the foundation for the AI-based visual reconstructions. We selected two case studies:

  • Mediterranean: Lake Martignano (Rome), focusing on the Late Glacial to Holocene transition.
  • Nordic: √Örsj√∂ (Southern Sweden), analysing the shift from pioneer vegetation to agriculture.

The images were generated using Gemini 3 Pro (specifically the Nano Banana iteration). Documentation and pollen data were sourced from the Neotoma Paleoecology Database.

Structure & How to Participate

The survey evaluates the botanical accuracy of the images. You can approach it at two levels:

  1. Quick review: Evaluate the reconstructions by observing the pollen diagrams provided on this page.
  2. Deep dive: Download and examine the raw CSV datasets and detailed prompts for a rigorous validation.
Select Your Case Study: You are not required to complete both sections. If a specific geographical context (Mediterranean vs. Nordic) lies outside your area of expertise, please feel free to skip that case study and focus only on the one where you feel most confident.

Thank you for your time and expertise.
